26 - 28 November, 2021

AKCSSC

All Kerala Computer Society Student Convention (AKCSSC) is the flagship event of the IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter, held enthusiastically every year. AKCSSC 2021 will be from November 26 - 28, 2021.

This year going with the theme 'Entrepreneurship and Digital Transformation', there are numerous events conducted in a hybrid model. AKCSSC provides a platform for all participants to foster technical prowess. It is an opportunity to meet and network with like-minded students and professionals which helps to gain experience and develop skills. The event is a blend of technical and cultural activities, thus exploring the limitless technology with a lot of fun.
