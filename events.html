Pre-Events

Digital Crossword Competition

As a pre-event to AKCSSC 2021, the IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter is organizing a Digital Crossword Competition. The event will be held on 18th November 2021 from 8:00 pm. The topic for the crossword competition is ‘Digital Transformation’ and it is conducted as the eighth task of Elever. All IEEE CS members of the Kerala Chapter can participate in this leaderboard competition.

GLITCH: AN ONLINE TREASURE HUNT

An online treasure hunt will be conducted by the College of Engineering, Adoor on 20th November 2021. Clues will be posted at regular intervals for the participants and each question will have a clue to the next question to solve the puzzle. The top three winners will be awarded tickets to attend the AKCSSC 2021 online.

Cre8ive: UI/UX designing competition

A UI/UX designing competition will be held by Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering as a pre-event to AKCSSC 2021. The event will be held from 14 - 17 November 2021. Students can participate individually or as a pair and the best team will be awarded a cash prize. The contestants have to: Identify the UI and UX problems associated with a popular mobile application and redesign the UI by ideating various solutions. A presentation containing the new UI and the reasons and research which led to this design submitted by the participant will be evaluated.
